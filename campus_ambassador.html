About the program

Reflux - The Annual Technical Symposium of the Department of Chemical Engineering, IIT Guwahati. Reflux campus ambassador program recognizes enthusiastic students, hardworking and motivated students by giving them opportunity to develop knowledge and leadership skills by representing Reflux on their campus.


Requirements

  • Enrolled in full time undergraduate/postgraduate in Chemical Engineering
  • Familiar with Social Networking Sites such as Facebook, Twitter
  • Involved with student organizations and other extra curricular Activities

Responsibilities

  • CA should act as a contact for Reflux in their Campus.
  • CA should bring at least 7 participation entries for Competitions to be eligible for the benefits.
  • CA should bring pre fest event participation from their campus. The first event is the online prelims of ENIGMA - National Level Chemical Quiz competition that is to be conducted on a particular date during the month of January.
  • Top 15 team will be selected for the next round at IIT-Guwahati. However the compensations will solely be the performance of the individual team at the National Level (in the First Round).
  • CA is responsible for promoting Reflux through Social media networks.
  • CA should not be biased with a student or a group of students.
    There are 3 groups based on your performance and goodies and other benefits will be decided totally on your performance in getting maximum participation.

Benefits

  • Get a certificate of Campus ambassador at Reflux
  • Campus ambassador can attend any paid workshop of reflux free of cost
  • Accommodation will be provided for free during Reflux
  • Get Reflux Merchandise such as T-shirts for free
  • Get personalized reflux ID cards
  • Reflect your position of responsibility & experience in your resume
  • ‘Campus Ambassador of the year’ Award will be up for grab.
  • Certificate of Excellence will be awarded to the Top 5 Campus Ambassadors. In case of any discrepancy, the decision of the faculty coordinator will be final and binding. Faculty Coordinator will be the sole judge of whether the CA has discharged his/her duties properly in line with the expectations from him/her.
