Enigma
Guidelines :
  • The quiz is conducted in 2 rounds - Online Prelims and National Finals
  • This is an individual quiz and each participation shall be separate
  • Registration fees for taking the quiz will be Rs 100 per individual
Online Prelims :
  • First Round is online and is open for everyone and shall be conducted on 11th February 2018* with 2 time slots 10-12 am* and 3-5 pm*
  • It is duty of Campus Ambassadors to bring the major participation for the prelims
  • Top 15 of Regional round will be invited for Nationals Round at IIT Guwahati between 16th -18th March
  • Questions in the quiz will be objective based
  • The questions will be based on aptitude and reasoning with a little flavor of chemical engineering
  • Any act of use of unfair means will lead to disqualification
National Finals :
  • There is no extra registration fee for National finals and the competition is conducted between top 15 of online prelims round
  • National Final of enigma is paper pen based offline exam and shall be conducted at IIT guwahati only between 16th -18th March
Rewards :
  • E certificate for each participant
  • Certificates and goodies* for top 15 of online prelims round
  • Top 15 shall participate in any one of the paid workshop without fee
  • Cash Prize and winner certificate for National Winners * (top 3)
    • *subject to change based on circumstances
